Fight Milk

The first alcoholic, dairy based, protein drink!

It contains essential vitamins and nutrients like crowtein to give YOU the strength to DOMINATE others.

Ingredients

  • 5 Crow Eggs (don't use chicken eggs, you'll need the crowtein from the crow eggs
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1 Cup of Vodka

Steps

  1. Crack the crow eggs into a pot.
  2. Whisk the eggs throughly.
  3. Add milk while whisking.
  4. Pour Vodka into the mixture and mix for 2 minutes.
  5. Bring pot to a simmer to activate the crowtein.
  6. Pour into container and refrigerate for and hour.
  7. Serve chilled before your fight.
